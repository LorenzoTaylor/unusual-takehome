## Design Context

### Users
Tech-savvy Head of Growth at a B2B SaaS startup. Analytical, ROI-obsessed, uses the tool during focused work sessions on desktop. Wants to understand exactly what changed and why — not just that it "sounds better."

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, cutting-edge AI, analytical. Three words: sharp, intelligent, precise.

### Aesthetic Direction
Black background. White primary text. Light grey subtext. #B8FA32 lime green for accents, buttons, active states. Axiforma SemiBold for headings/labels, Axiforma Regular for body. Fallback: Jost from Google Fonts. All icons from Lucide.

Feel: "a Notion doc that got smart." Single column, document-like, no dashboard chrome. Results appear inline as the document grows — not in modals or side panels. Flat layout — no nested cards.

Anti-references: no glassmorphism, no cyan/purple AI gradients, no rounded card grids, no hero metric dashboards.

### Design Principles
1. Document-first: the UI is a smart document, not an app with panels
2. Accent is precious: #B8FA32 used only on CTAs, active states, and high-signal moments
3. Results are first-class: the output section is as visually deliberate as the input
4. Every field earns its place: no decorative chrome, no redundant labels
5. Trust through specificity: show the reasoning, not just the answer
